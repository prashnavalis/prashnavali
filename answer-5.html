Meaning

O Lord! You are the supreme teacher and the bestower of wisdom. Your devotees who seek knowledge with devotion receive divine wisdom. You are the giver of all knowledge and the destroyer of ignorance.

Interpretation

This chaupai reveals that Lord Shri Rama is the supreme teacher who grants wisdom to His devotees. He destroys ignorance and bestows divine knowledge. This answer indicates that you should seek knowledge with devotion and the Lord will guide you to wisdom.
