Meaning

O Lord! You are the supreme master of all. Your devotees who chant Your name with devotion attain liberation. You are the giver of all auspiciousness and the destroyer of all sorrows.

Interpretation

This chaupai reveals that Lord Shri Rama is the supreme master of everything. He grants liberation to His devotees and destroys all sorrows. This answer indicates that your devotion will lead to ultimate liberation and all your sorrows will be removed.
