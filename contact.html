Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ram Prashnavali work?

+

Ram Prashnavali is based on a 15x15 grid made up of nine chaupais from the Ramcharitmanas. You ask your question and click on any square, which gives you a divine answer.

Is it free?

+

Yes, Ram Prashnavali is completely free. Our mission is to provide access to Lord Shri Rama's divine wisdom to all devotees.

Can I ask the same question multiple times?

+

No, it is not recommended to ask the same question multiple times. You should use the prashnavali only once for your question.

Does it work on mobile?

+

Yes, our website is fully mobile-friendly. You can use Ram Prashnavali from any device.
