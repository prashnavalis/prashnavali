Meaning

O Lord! You are the supreme protector and the ultimate refuge. Your devotees who surrender to You find complete protection and eternal peace. You are the destroyer of all fears and the giver of courage.

Interpretation

This chaupai reveals that Lord Shri Rama is the supreme protector of His devotees. He destroys all fears and provides complete protection to those who surrender to Him. This answer assures you that you are under divine protection and should have no fear.
