Meaning

O Lord! Your glory is supreme. You are the destroyer of all sins and the giver of salvation. Those who remember You with devotion attain eternal peace and happiness.

Interpretation

This chaupai reveals that Lord Shri Rama's glory is beyond measure. He is the ultimate destroyer of all sins and the supreme giver of salvation. This answer indicates that your path is blessed and you should continue with faith and devotion.
