Our Story

The beginning of Ram Prashnavali started with a simple idea. I noticed that in today's digital age, people need spiritual guidance, but it has become difficult to access traditional methods.

The Ram Shalaka Prashnavali described in the Ramcharitmanas is a sacred and powerful tool that has been helping devotees for centuries. I thought why not make it available in digital form so that people around the world can benefit from it.

Our team has reconstructed the traditional 15x15 grid in digital form, making it mobile-friendly and user-friendly. We have ensured that spiritual authenticity remains and the user experience is better.
